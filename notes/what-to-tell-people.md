# What to Tell People (TLDR)

## The one-liner

> I'm building a grocery app for people on a cut — scan a protein bar or yogurt, get an instant buy/skip answer, and see why.

## If they want 10 more seconds

> You know how you stand in Target comparing labels for 2 minutes? This tells you in 5 seconds if it fits your goals — protein, sugar, calories — and helps you pick between two options. Starting with protein bars and Greek yogurt for fitness shoppers.

## Why it's different from Yuka

> Yuka tells you if food is "healthy." This tells you if it's right for *your* goal right now — cutting, hitting protein, avoiding sugar — and why you'd put it back on the shelf.

## Casual (friends)

> It's like having a macro-savvy friend in the grocery aisle. Scan it, get buy/maybe/skip, move on.
